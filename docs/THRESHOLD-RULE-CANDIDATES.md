# Threshold Rule Candidates

Accessed: 2026-06-13

## Purpose

These are starter deterministic rule candidates for the Queensland wedge. They are not a complete rules corpus. They are only the first rule fragments directly supported by currently reviewed official sources.

## State Baseline

### Queensland accepted development baseline

Business Queensland states that some minor work is accepted development under the Building Regulation 2021 and that owners still need to comply with applicable standards and local planning schemes.

Source:

- <https://www.business.qld.gov.au/industries/building-property-development/building-construction/approvals-inspections/when-not-needed>

## Brisbane City Council

### Deck starter candidate

Likely no building approval when all of the following are true:

- plan area no more than 10 square metres
- height no more than 1 metre above natural ground
- not roofed
- no side longer than 5 metres
- no structural integrity impact on existing buildings, retaining walls, or adjoining structures
- no alteration to pool enclosure or pool fence

Source:

- <https://www.brisbane.qld.gov.au/building-and-planning/getting-started-on-your-project/residential-projects/deck>

### Shed starter candidate

Likely no building approval when all of the following are true:

- plan area no more than 10 square metres
- maximum height no more than 2.4 metres
- mean height no more than 2.1 metres
- no side longer than 5 metres
- no structural integrity impact on existing buildings, retaining walls, or adjoining structures
- no alteration to pool enclosure or pool fence

Source:

- <https://www.brisbane.qld.gov.au/building-and-planning/getting-started-on-your-project/residential-projects/shed>

## Sunshine Coast Council

### Deck, pergola, verandah, patio and balcony starter candidate

Building work approval is indicated when any of the following are exceeded:

- 10 square metres in area
- mean height 2.1 metres and maximum height 2.4 metres
- 5 metres in length on any side

Source:

- <https://www.sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au/development/building/what-needs-building-approval>

### Shed starter candidate

Likely no building work approval when all of the following are true:

- plan area no more than 10 square metres
- no side longer than 5 metres
- maximum height no more than 2.4 metres
- mean height no more than 2.1 metres
- complies with minimum boundary setbacks
- not located within a registered easement
- no load on adjacent retaining wall, council stormwater service, or sewer

Source:

- <https://www.sunshinecoast.qld.gov.au/development/building/sheds>
